## üîß Troubleshooting

**If you get "No tickers specified":**
- Add ticker symbols: "Show me a pie chart of AAPL, MSFT revenue"
- Or use sector: "Show me a pie chart of tech company revenue"

**If you get "No data available":**
- Check that the tickers are correct
- Try different companies
- Make sure the metric exists (revenue, profit, etc.)

**If chart doesn't generate:**
- Try rephrasing with explicit tickers
- Use simpler query: "Show me a pie chart of AAPL, MSFT revenue"
- Check that you included a metric (revenue, profit, etc.)
